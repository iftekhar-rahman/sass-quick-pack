About us

Group History

Incorporation as a technical support for ship building

Keyfield Offshore Sdn Bhd (“KOSB”), our principal company, was incorporated and commenced its business of providing technical support and advisory services for shipbuilding.

2013

Vessel Chartering

We ventured into AWB chartering and had secured our first chartering contract using a third party AWB.

2016

Delivery of Laguna Setia 1

Our Group took delivery of our very first own AWB, Laguna Setia 1. She had secured her first chartering contract and commenced operations in the same year.

2017

License

KOSB applied from PETRONAS and subsequently obtained the PETRONAS Vessel Owner-Operator license which enables the company to be invited for projects by oil and gas contractors

2018

Umbrella Contract

Petronas Carigali Sdn Bhd (“PCSB”) awarded KOSB a Letter of Award to be a panel contractor under the Umbrella Contract for Offshore Support Vessel Services for PCSB’s drilling and project activities. Secured chartering contracts to charter out LS1 and a third party AWB under the Umbrella Contract.

2019

Delivery of Laguna Setia

Our Group took delivery of our second AWB, Laguna Setia 2, sister vessel to Laguna Setia 1.
